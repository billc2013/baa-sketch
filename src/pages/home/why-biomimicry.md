## Why Biomimicry?

### *“What if, every time I started to invent something, I asked, ‘How would nature solve this?”* — Janine Benyus

- Integrated Science, Engineering, and Technology 
- Enables teachers’ design capacity to adapt integrated STEM curriculum that connects science, engineering, and technology through the study of nature 
- Mirror the authentic work of scientists, engineers, and roboticists 
- “What is a better engineer than nature? It’s been doing this for six billion years.” - Erik 
- NGSS crosscutting concept – structure and function 
- Adaptable to a very wide range of authentic design challenges
