# About the Project

**Biomimicry as an Authentic Anchor (BAA)** is an NSF-funded collaboration between
TERC and Tufts University. Our goal is to help middle-school science teachers
integrate biomimicry-anchored engineering design and computational thinking into
their classrooms.

## Funding
